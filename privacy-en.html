Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 29, 2026

1. Who we are

The data controllers are José Ramón Bendicho Flores, Manuel Jiménez Carmona and Andrea Padilla Vallés (hereinafter "We" or "Planeo"). We operate the Planeo mobile application, a platform for discovering and creating social experiences. For any enquiry, please contact us at support@planeoeo.com.

2. Our values and why you should read this Notice

We value your privacy and want to be responsible, fair and transparent in the way we collect and process your Personal Data. This notice explains what information we collect, how we use it and your rights when using the Planeo App.

3. Minors

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Under our terms, users must be over 18 years of age to use the Services independently. If we detect that a user is under 13, we will immediately delete their account and all associated data.

4. What personal data do we collect and use?

We collect the following data to operate the App:

  • Information you provide: First and last name, email address, gender, date of birth and profile photo.
  • Location: If you enable location services, we track your location via GPS technology to show you nearby experiences.
  • Technical data: IP address, access logs, device type and information about your interaction with the platform.
  • Social networks: If you sign in with Google, we will collect information from your public profile as authorised by those platforms.
  • Camera: We request access to your device camera for two purposes: (1) to scan QR codes during event check-in (Host feature only) and (2) to take photos for your profile picture, event images or posts. The camera is only activated when you explicitly initiate one of these actions. No video or image is recorded or stored without your knowledge.
  • Photo library: We request access to your photo library to allow you to select existing images from your device to use as a profile picture, event cover image or post content. We only access the images you explicitly select.
  • Calendar: We request access to your device's native calendar solely to allow you to add events you have joined directly from the App. We do not read, modify or delete any pre-existing events in your calendar.

5. How do we use your Personal Data?

We process your data on the following legal grounds:

  • Performance of a contract: To provide our Services, manage your account and respond to your enquiries.
  • Legitimate interest: To detect and prevent fraud, maintain system security and personalise your experience by suggesting plans similar to your interests.
  • Consent: For the use of your real-time GPS location and the sending of promotional communications.
  • Legal obligation: To comply with tax regulations or requirements from authorities.

6. How do we use your Personal Data for marketing?

6.1 Experience discovery and recommendation

We use your contact details and activity to suggest relevant plans on the platform. You will receive communications if you have requested information or registered in the App.

6.2 Third-party marketing

At this time, Planeo does not share your data with third parties for external marketing purposes.

6.3 Opt-out

You can adjust your communication preferences or unsubscribe at any time through the App or the link in our emails.

6.4 Third-party advertising companies

We currently do not include third-party advertising on our platform.

7. How we share your Personal Data

  • Service providers: With third parties that help us deliver the service (data hosting, analytics).
  • Event organisers: When you join a plan, the organiser will see your name and profile photo to manage attendance.
  • Legal compliance: Where necessary to investigate a crime, prevent fraud or comply with court orders.

8. International data transfers

As a general rule, we store your data within the European Economic Area (EEA). If any technical provider requires the transfer of data outside the EEA, we ensure that standard contractual clauses approved by the European Commission are in place.

9. Cookies and tracking technologies

We use cookies to enable security features, remember your language preferences and analyse App performance. You can control or disable the use of cookies through your device settings.

10. Security

We apply technical and organisational measures to protect your data. However, no Internet transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee the absolute security of transmitted data.

11. Data retention and deletion

11.1 Retention: We will retain your data while your account is active and as necessary for the purposes described. We may delete your account and data if you do not use the Services for a period of two years.

11.2 Account deletion from the App In compliance with Apple App Store Guidelines (Guideline 5.1.1), you can request the permanent deletion of your account and all your personal data directly from the application: Profile → Settings → Delete account

Once the request is initiated:

  • Your personal identifying data (name, email, profile photo) will be permanently deleted within a maximum of 30 days.
  • Published content (events, posts, reviews) may be anonymised rather than deleted where necessary to preserve the integrity of the platform.
  • Data required by legal obligation (e.g. tax records) may be retained for the legally required period before final deletion.

11.3 Request by email: If for any reason you cannot access the App, you may also request deletion of your account by emailing support@planeoeo.com.

12. Third-party personal data

If you provide us with data about other attendees (friends or family), you warrant that you have informed them about this privacy policy.

13. Your rights and how to exercise them

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Organic Law 3/2018 (LOPDGDD), you have the right to:

  • Access: Obtain information about how we use your data and request a copy.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request the deletion of your personal data (including account deletion as described in section 11.2).
  • Objection and Restriction: Object to processing based on legitimate interest or restrict its use.
  • Portability: Request that we transfer your data to another company in a readable format.

To exercise these rights, you can do so directly from the App or by emailing support@planeoeo.com.

14. Third-party sites

Our App may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy policies of third parties once you leave our platform.

15. Changes to this Privacy Notice

This notice was last updated in January 2026. We will notify you of any significant changes through the App or by email.

16. Complaints, questions and suggestions

For any queries about your data, you can contact us at support@planeoeo.com.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (AEPD) at www.aepd.es.
